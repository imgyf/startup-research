# Target Audience

**Phase:** 3 — Research (Wave 3 synthesis)
**Project:** ai-website-sg-restaurants
**Date:** 2026-05-11
**Confidence:** Medium (SG-specific persona splits are estimates; need 5-10 owner interviews to lock in)

---

## Primary Persona — "Soft-Stretched Owner-Operator"

**Snapshot:**
- Independent (single-outlet or 2-3-outlet) restaurant, cafe, or bar
- Owner is 35-55 years old, in the venue 5-7 days/week
- Runs day-to-day ops; "marketing" is them or a family member posting to Instagram when they remember
- Monthly P&L: SGD 40-150k revenue, 3-8% net margin = SGD 1.5-12k profit
- Already pays 15-35% commission on FoodPanda/Grab orders [Data, Tier 2]
- Has a Google Business listing, an Instagram, maybe a Facebook page
- Either no website, a stale 2015-era one, or a half-built Wix they gave up on

**JTBD ("Job to be Done"):**

> "Help me look professional on Google when somebody searches for me, so I don't lose the reservation/walk-in/order to whoever's listed above me — without me having to learn how to run a website."

That's the actual job. Notice what it's NOT:
- It's NOT "build me a website" (that's our solution)
- It's NOT "I need AI" (they don't care about AI)
- It's NOT "improve my SEO" (they don't know that word)

## Pain Hierarchy

Ordered by which pain actually drives buying decisions:

| Rank | Pain | Vocal? | What customer says |
|------|------|--------|---------------------|
| 1 | "I'm losing reservations / walk-ins to better-ranked competitors on Google" | **Latent** — they don't see it | "Why is [neighbor restaurant] always full and we're not?" |
| 2 | "FoodPanda/Grab is eating my margin" | Vocal | "These platforms take 25% — siao [crazy]" |
| 3 | "My existing website looks bad / is broken / I can't update it" | Mid | "My old web guy disappeared" |
| 4 | "I don't have time to do marketing" | Vocal | "Got too many things to do already" |
| 5 | "I want to look professional like the chains" | Mid | "We're not just an Instagram restaurant" |
| 6 | "Phone photos look bad" | Vocal | "I don't have nice pictures" |

**[Opinion]** The highest-leverage sales angle is **#1 (Google ranking loss) and #2 (FoodPanda margin recovery).** Both are quantifiable in dollars. "You're losing ~SGD 600/month in reservations to [Competitor X] who outranks you, and giving away SGD 1,800/month to FoodPanda in commissions. Our SGD 1,500 site pays for itself in 6 weeks." That's a closeable pitch.

The wedge is **not** "you don't have a website." It's "**here's what your website should look like, and here's what it would do for your bottom line.**"

## Language Map (Owner Words → Industry Jargon)

| What the owner says | What we shouldn't say |
|---------------------|------------------------|
| "Come up in Google" | "SEO" |
| "Menu" | "Content management" |
| "Reservations" | "Booking flow" |
| "Promote" | "Marketing automation" |
| "Cheaper, no hidden cost" | "Subscription model" |
| "Run by my niece" | "We have a marketing team" |
| "Update menu" | "CMS access" |
| "Take orders" | "E-commerce checkout" |
| "Cannot work la" | "Sub-optimal conversion funnel" |

Sales material, email subject lines, and the spec-site preview itself should use the left column. Anything in the right column kills trust.

## Decision Criteria (Inferred Priority)

When evaluating our offer, owners weigh (highest first):

1. **Trust** — Is this person real? SG-based? Local face/name? Portfolio?
2. **Price + no hidden recurring cost** (or PSG-eligible)
3. **Speed to live** — "I want it next week"
4. **The shown sample of MY restaurant** — the wedge, makes it real
5. **Updates after launch** — who maintains it
6. **Domain ownership** — "Will I own my own .com / .sg?"

## Common Objections (To Pre-Empt in the Spec-Site Pitch)

| Objection | Pre-emptive response |
|-----------|----------------------|
| "Already on Instagram, why need this?" | "Google search != Instagram. Show their actual Google search result page — most look bad." |
| "I tried Wix, too complicated" | "You don't have to touch it. We build, you approve, we ship." |
| "How much is the monthly?" | "SGD 0/month for the basic plan. Hosting + domain SGD 25/month if you want us to handle it." |
| "Is this PSG eligible?" | **Honest answer for v1: "Not yet — we're applying. So this is the unsubsidized price."** OR (after approval): "Yes — you pay SGD 450 after 70% subsidy." |
| "Can it take orders?" | "We link to your existing Oddle/FoodPanda/Grab. Building you a new ordering checkout would cost SGD 5k+ — not worth it." |
| "Who will maintain it?" | "Pay-as-you-go updates SGD 50 each, or SGD 50/month for unlimited menu updates and reviews response. Your call." |

## Persona Split (Working Hypothesis — Validate via Interviews)

| Sub-persona | Estimated share of SG independents | Buying disposition |
|-------------|------------------------------------|---------------------|
| Owner does it all on phone (no website) | 50-60% | Best fit — most acute pain, easiest close |
| Family member helps with IG (no website) | 20-25% | Good fit — family member is co-buyer |
| Hired part-time freelancer (IG-only or stale site) | 10-15% | Mid fit — they have inertia |
| Agency-managed (chains, hotels) | 5-10% | Out of scope — wrong ACV |

## Where to Reach Them

| Channel | Effectiveness for this persona | Founder action |
|---------|--------------------------------|-----------------|
| **Walk into the restaurant during 2-4pm lull** | **Highest** — owner physically present | Tablet + pre-built site demo |
| Email `info@restaurant.com.sg` | Mid — variable inbox monitoring | 3-4x lift from pre-built link |
| WhatsApp / IG DM | Mid-high — owners read these | Build a real, populated business account first |
| LinkedIn | Low for independents, OK for multi-outlet | Skip for v1 |
| Hawker/coffeeshop in-person | Wrong ICP — different buyer | Skip for v1 |
| RAS / industry events | High concentration, slow | Pursue after first 20 customers |

## Flags

**Red Flags:**
- None identified beyond the latent-pain risk: if owners genuinely don't feel the loss, no amount of clever cold outreach will close them.

**Yellow Flags:**
- **Latent pain = longer close cycles.** Owners must be *shown* the loss (Google search result side-by-side, FoodPanda commission math). The spec site alone may not be enough; we likely also need a 90-second "your current online presence vs. what it could be" diagnostic.
- **Persona split is unvalidated** — the 50-60% "owner does it all" estimate is from analog France data. SG could split differently (e.g., more family-member involvement).
- **PSG-eligibility question is a closing objection.** Until the agency is PSG-approved, every grant-eligible prospect has a real reason to choose Verz/OOm instead. The early funnel must filter for non-PSG-eligible or PSG-indifferent owners.
