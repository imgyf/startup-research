# 30-Day Action Plan

**Phase:** Final deliverable
**Project:** pokemon-champions-team-workspace
**Date:** 2026-05-10
**Principle:** **Month 1 is NOT a build month.** It's customer discovery + advisor + creator + prototype validation. Build commits at Week 6 only after scorecard conditions are validated.

---

## Big Picture

The four scorecard conditions for proceeding to MVP build:

1. ≥10/15 customer interviews validate the pain framing (E1)
2. A credible competitive-player advisor signs on (E2)
3. At least one of WolfeyVGC / CybertronVGC / Brady Smith is in active partnership conversation (E3)
4. Wizard-of-oz coach validates the differentiation (E7)

Month 1 stands up the conditions for assessing all four by Week 6.
