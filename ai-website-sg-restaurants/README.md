# AI Website Agency for SG Restaurants — Startup Design Summary

**Project:** ai-website-sg-restaurants
**Date:** 2026-05-11
**Mode:** Fast Track (compressed phases due to lean budget)
**Verdict:** **CONDITIONAL PROCEED** — Score 5.5/10

---

## One-Paragraph Overview

An AI-native website-building agency for Singapore SMB restaurants. The differentiator is **pre-built spec sites** delivered during cold outreach — instead of pitching "we can build you a website," prospects are shown a working preview of their own restaurant's website before being asked to commit. Pricing SGD 1,000-1,500 one-time + optional SGD 79/mo maintenance. Two-person bootstrapped team, <SGD 5k starting capital, walk-in-first sales motion in dense SG neighborhoods (Joo Chiat, Tanjong Pagar, Tiong Bahru). The thesis: AI compresses spec-site cost to near-zero, so "show first, sell second" becomes economically viable at SMB scale.

## Key Findings

1. **The wedge has external data behind it.** Restaurant pitches with specific online-visibility diagnostics close at 40% vs 10% for generic pitches [DataLane, Tier 2]; personalized outreach gets 4× the reply rate [FirstSales, Tier 2]. This is the strongest piece of evidence in the entire research and the load-bearing premise of the business.

2. **Market is real but structurally constrained.** ~27-30k addressable SG outlets, ~10-18k beachhead independents without owned websites [Estimate]. Realistic Year-1 SOM = SGD 100-270k revenue for a 2-person team — a viable bootstrap, not a venture-scale market.

3. **PSG is the strategic fulcrum.** Singapore's Productivity Solutions Grant rose to 70% support from 1 April 2026. Competitors who are PSG-approved (Verz Design, OOm, MediaPlus, Orfeo Story) effectively price their SGD 5k sites at SGD 1,500 to grant-eligible customers — matching our unsubsidized list price with stronger trust. **Becoming PSG-approved is mandatory long-term, not optional.**

4. **AI website tooling is commoditizing in 6-18 months.** Wix Harmony/Aria, Squarespace Blueprint, Durable, 10Web, Lovable, v0, Bolt — all 2024-2025 entrants. The "AI-native" marketing claim is a wasting asset; durable advantage must come from local operator labor, SG-specific integrations (PayNow, Oddle, Chope), and PSG approval.

5. **Pain is latent, not vocal.** SG restaurant owners don't complain about not having a website — they silently lose reservations to better-ranked competitors. The cold pitch must *show* the loss (Google search side-by-side, FoodPanda commission math) before the spec site is shown.

6. **Walk-ins beat cold email at this ACV.** F&B owners are uniquely physically present in their venues 6-7 days/week — no other B2B vertical has this property. Walk-in close rate estimated at 5% (3-6 customers/week/founder); cold email viable only if per-site compute is ≤SGD 2.

7. **Unit economics work, but founder draw is lean.** 75-80% gross margin at SGD 1,200 ACV. Break-even at 10 closes/month. Founder draw caps at SGD 4k each through month 3 in the base case. SGD 5k starting cash absorbs the month-1 loss only if ≥4 closes hit.

## Strategic Positioning Summary

> **"See your restaurant's new website tonight. Pay SGD 1,500 to ship it next week. No monthly fee unless you want one."**

Bottom-left of the market positioning map: low-ACV, full-service, SG-local, AI-native. Differentiated against:
- **Horizontal AI builders (Wix/Squarespace/Durable):** by doing the operator labor for the owner
- **Local SG agencies (Verz/OOm/MediaPlus):** by speed, price, and AI-native cost structure
- **Delivery aggregators (FoodPanda/Grab):** by reclaiming commission and owning the customer relationship
- **Vertical SaaS (Oddle/Chope):** by full brand site, not microsite — partnership-friendly, not competitive

## Top 3 Risks and Mitigations

1. **Spec-site wedge may not lift in SG (M×4 = 12).** Mitigation: Experiments 2 and 4 explicitly test this in month 1. Don't scale beyond pilot until validated.

2. **Latent pain blocks cold close (M×4 = 12).** Mitigation: Diagnostic-first pitch ("here's what you're losing") before showing the spec site. Validate via Experiment 3 no-sale interviews.

3. **AI commoditization (M×3 = 9).** Mitigation: Move value claim from "AI-built" (feature) to "Google-ranked outcome." Apply for PSG approval in parallel. Build SG-specific integration moats (PayNow, Oddle handshake).

## Confidence Snapshot (What We Know vs. What We're Guessing)

**Solid data:** PSG mechanics, F&B closure rates, SME digital adoption, competitor pricing tiers, AI tooling costs, cold-email legal status, restaurant tech spend priorities. (All Tier 1-2.)

**Soft data:** Persona splits, walk-in close rate, cold-email reply rate, per-site compute cost in real production, owner price sensitivity. (Mostly Tier 2/3, US-based, requires SG-specific validation.)

**Open questions:** % of SG independents without owned websites, PSG eligibility for AI-generated deliverables, whether maintenance attach holds at 30% in SG.

See `01-discovery/confidence-dashboard.md` for the full audit.

## Anti-Patterns Detected

- **None active.** This is not a "solution looking for a problem" — the customer pain is real, just latent. It's not "boiling the ocean" — the SG-restaurants beachhead is appropriately narrow. It's not "premature scaling" — the validation plan is correctly cheap and time-boxed. **One yellow signal:** the "AI-native" positioning has light "solution-led" tendency; the recommended reframe to outcome-led ("Google-ranked, FoodPanda-saved") corrects this.

## Final Recommendation

**Run the 30-day validation sprint.** Cost: ~SGD 800 + one month. Outcome: real-data answer to "is this worth committing to." If the gates pass, scale aggressively into months 2-3 and apply for PSG. If they don't, the loss is small and the learning is valuable for the next idea.

The honest answer to "is this company worth building?" is: **worth testing, not yet worth committing to.** The data supports proceeding to the test; the test supports proceeding to the commitment. Don't skip the middle step.

**Yellow Flags:**

- One-time pricing caps growth without maintenance attach ≥20%.
