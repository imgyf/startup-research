## Direct Wedge Defensibility Analysis

**Our claim:** Pre-built spec sites drive 4× cold-outreach response and ~40% close rate (vs 10% baseline) [Data, DataLane / FirstSales studies — Tier 2/3, US-based].

**Who else can do this?**

| Competitor | Can they spec-site cold outreach? | Why they don't / haven't |
|------------|----------------------------------|--------------------------|
| Verz / OOm / MediaPlus | Yes, technically — but their cost structure makes spec work uneconomic at scale (SGD 100+ labor cost per spec) | High labor cost; they rely on inbound + PSG-driven inquiries |
| Wix / Squarespace / Durable | They literally do this (generate from prompt) but DIY — they don't *deliver* a sample to the prospect, the prospect must build it themselves | Product-led model; cold sales is not their motion |
| Subraa / freelancers | Could, but solo capacity caps it | One-person bandwidth |
| AI-native foreign agencies | Not surfaced in 2025-2026 search; some exist in the US/UK | No SG localization, no on-the-ground sales |

**[Opinion] Defensibility window: 12-18 months.** Wix Aria and similar agents are racing toward "we'll cold-pitch your prospects with a sample site we generated for them." Once Wix bundles cold-outreach automation into a SGD 30/month plan, the agency wedge collapses to "we'll do the operator labor for you" — still a real service, but no longer unique.

## Platform Risk

- **Lovable / v0 / Bolt** are our hammer. If their pricing rises or quality degrades, costs spike. **Mitigation:** maintain proficiency in 2-3 tools; export to plain HTML/React where possible.
- **PSG vendor approval criteria** could tighten to exclude AI-generated deliverables. **Mitigation:** ensure human review + customization on every delivered site so it's clearly a service, not a tool output.
- **Wix AI / Squarespace AI** improving fast — they could ship "AI cold-outreach for restaurants" in 2026-2027. **Mitigation:** move up-market into managed services before the floor drops.

## Vulnerability Map (Where Competitors Are Weak)

1. **PSG-approved agencies** are slow (weeks-to-months timelines), formal, and don't do spec work. **Our exploit:** "Spec site in 48 hours, paid site in 7 days."
2. **Horizontal AI builders** require the restaurant owner to drive the AI — most don't have time. **Our exploit:** "We do it for you."
3. **Delivery aggregators** burn 15-35% commissions forever. **Our exploit:** "Your site pays for itself in saved commissions in the first month."
4. **Oddle / Chope** lock the restaurant into their platform branding. **Our exploit:** "Own your customer relationship, embed Oddle ordering into your own site."

## Flags

**Red Flags:**
- **PSG-approved competitors at 70% subsidy effectively match our unsubsidized list price** while bringing trust + government endorsement. This is a structural pricing problem, not a marketing problem.
- **Wix Aria / Durable can already generate a site in seconds** — the "we generated a site for you" novelty is fading. The agency-grade *service* (not the tool output) is the real moat.

**Yellow Flags:**
- **Several SG vertical brands (FoodMatch, FoodieSquare, GoFood) failed to surface in 2026 search** — possibly defunct. Could mean the segment has consolidated to Oddle + Chope only, or could mean cannibalization risk we missed.
- **No AI-native SG agency competitor surfaced.** Either greenfield (good) or we missed them in 6-10 searches (concerning) — founder should run direct LinkedIn + Carousell + Telegram searches for "AI website Singapore restaurant" before assuming first-mover advantage.
