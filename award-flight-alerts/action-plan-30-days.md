# 30-Day Action Plan

**Date:** 2026-05-09
**Goal:** Decide — with evidence — whether to (a) stop, (b) pivot to one of the three open angles, or (c) build the literal idea anyway because the founder has an unfair advantage not yet disclosed.

**Total budget:** ~$540 ad spend + ~30 hours of founder time.

---

## Week 1 — Customer Discovery (Cost: ~$240, ~10 hours)

**Objective:** Verify whether the *strategic-insight* pain (the most-promising pivot) is real and worth paying for.

- **Day 1.** Post one structured research thread in `r/awardtravel` (mod-permitted) and `r/churning`: "Researching award-search tool gaps — paying $20 for a 20-min Zoom." Use a throwaway email or a personal account, not a brand.
- **Day 1.** Post the same in 1 FlyerTalk thread + 2 Discord servers (Award Travel 101, /r/churning Discord).
- **Day 2–3.** Filter respondents: must be users of ≥1 paid award-search tool, must have redeemed ≥3 award flights in past 12 months. Aim for 15 confirmed, schedule 12.
- **Day 4–7.** Run interviews. Use the script in `06-validation/scorecard.md`, Experiment 1. **Do not pitch your idea.** Listen.
- **Day 7.** Write up: did ≥7/12 describe the strategic-insight pain unprompted? Did ≥5/12 say they'd pay $50+/yr for an insight tool?

**Decision gate end of Week 1:**
- ❌ <5 say they'd pay → **stop the pivot thesis**, evaluate the B2B-travel-advisor or non-US pivots instead, or stop entirely.
- ✅ ≥5 say they'd pay → continue to Week 2.

---

## Week 2 — Landing-Page Smoke Test (Cost: $300 ads, ~6 hours)

**Objective:** Test if the AI-insight pivot can attract paid traffic at viable CAC.

- **Day 8.** Build a landing page on Carrd or Framer (~3 hours):
  - Headline: "Stop guessing if your award redemption is a good deal."
  - Subhead: "We tell you which transfer partner to use, why this seat is a sweet spot, and when the price will move."
  - Hero CTA: "Get early access — first 100 free for life." Email capture only.
  - Below the fold: 3 problem-statements, 3 example outputs (mocked screenshots), founder's name and a sentence on what you've redeemed personally.
- **Day 9.** Set up Reddit Ads ($150) targeting `r/awardtravel`, `r/churning`, `r/travel`. Set up Meta Ads ($150) targeting "Chase Sapphire Reserve" / "Amex Platinum" interest in 25–55 age range, US.
- **Day 9–14.** Run 7 days. Monitor daily. Pause and re-write if click-through is below 1.5% by day 4.
- **Day 14.** Total: clicks, signups, replies to welcome email, % who answered "Would you pay $X for this?" in the welcome email's single-question survey.

**Decision gate end of Week 2:**
- ❌ <100 signups OR CAC > $5/email → **stop**.
- ✅ ≥100 signups, CAC ≤$3/email, ≥5 replies indicating willingness to pay → continue to Week 3.

---

## Week 3 — Wizard-of-Oz Manual Service Test (Cost: $0, ~10 hours)

**Objective:** Test willingness to pay actual money for the *insight* (not the software).

- **Day 15.** Email the top 30 leads from Week 2 (those who replied to the welcome email).
- Offer: *"As a thanks for your interest, I'm running a private beta. For $39, I'll send you a custom report within 48 hours: your 3 best award redemptions given your points balances and travel goals. I'm doing this manually right now — software comes later. Reply if interested."*
- **Day 16–21.** Take 10 paying users. Spend up to 1 hour per report. Use Seats.aero, point.me, your own knowledge. Deliver via PDF.
- **Day 21.** Follow up: "Would you pay $39 again next month?" "Was this useful?" "What would have made it 10×?"

**Decision gate end of Week 3:**
- ❌ <3/10 pay → **stop**. The insight pain is a real complaint but not real money.
- ❌ <2/10 say they'd pay again → **stop** or pivot pricing model.
- ✅ ≥4 pay AND ≥2 would pay again AND each report took ≤1 hour → **strong signal**, continue to Week 4.

---

## Week 4 — Decision and Next-Phase Planning (Cost: $0, ~4 hours)

**Objective:** Make the irreversible decision, with evidence.

- **Day 22.** Re-read all 3 weeks' results in one session. Be honest: did each gate pass cleanly, or did you talk yourself into the next gate?
- **Day 23.** Pick one of three outcomes:

  **Outcome A — Strong signal (all 3 gates passed cleanly):**
  - Re-run startup design in **Full Mode** with the new pivot framing ("AI award redemption insight tool"), not the literal mobile-alerts framing.
  - Begin Phase 5 (Brand) and Phase 6 (Product) for the new framing.
  - Decide: do you raise pre-seed ($250–500K) to support 6–12 months of build, or bootstrap with concierge revenue while building?

  **Outcome B — Mixed signal (2/3 gates passed, or all 3 passed with caveats):**
  - Run a 30-day extension: convert the manual service into a $79/month subscription. If 5+ subscribe and 3+ retain past month 2, proceed to Outcome A. Else, treat as Outcome C.

  **Outcome C — Negative signal (any gate failed):**
  - Stop. Document what was learned. The hours spent are the price of *not* spending 12 months building software no one wants.
  - Consider: do the open angles (B2B travel-advisor, non-US focus) justify a fresh validation cycle? If so, run a new 30-day plan for one of those.
  - Otherwise, **the right answer is to find a different problem.** This is not a personal failure; this is the system working.

- **Day 24–28.** Execute on the chosen outcome. If Outcome C, write a one-page post-mortem and move on.

## What This Plan Deliberately Does NOT Do

- ❌ Build a mobile app
- ❌ Set up scraping infrastructure
- ❌ Register a company
- ❌ Design a logo / brand
- ❌ Write a pitch deck
- ❌ Reach out to investors
- ❌ Build a landing page that says "mobile app for award alerts" (because that idea is already invalidated)

Everything in this plan is reversible and the **total cost is under $600**. The cost of not doing this validation first is months of building the wrong thing.
