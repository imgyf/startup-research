# Lean Canvas

**Phase:** 4 — Strategy (Fast Track: Lean Canvas only)
**Project:** ai-website-sg-restaurants
**Date:** 2026-05-11
**Confidence:** Medium (rests on validation outcomes in Phase 8)

---

## Refined Concept (Recommended)

**AI-built restaurant websites for SG independents, delivered with a pre-built spec site as the cold-outreach hook.** Pricing: **SGD 1,000-1,500 one-time** with optional **SGD 79/month maintenance** add-on. Beachhead: independent restaurants/cafes/bars in dense SG neighborhoods (Tanjong Pagar, Joo Chiat, Tiong Bahru, Telok Ayer, Bugis). Sales motion: **walk-ins with tablet demo** in months 1-3, layering in cold email when revenue funds compute. Strategic move: **apply for PSG vendor approval in parallel** to convert grant-eligible inbound at SGD 900 effective price.

## The Canvas

### 1. Problem (Top 3 — ranked by buying urgency)
1. **Lost reservations to better-ranked competitors on Google** (latent, must be shown via diagnostic)
2. **Paying 15-35% commissions on FoodPanda/Grab forever** (vocal, quantifiable in dollars)
3. **Current website looks bad / broken / can't update** (mid — affects pride and trust)

### 2. Customer Segments
- **Primary:** Owner-operator, 35-55, single-outlet independent SG restaurant/cafe/bar
- **Secondary:** 2-3 outlet operators (multi-location independents, not chains)
- **Out of scope for v1:** Chains, hotel restaurants, hawkers, foreign-owned outlets (PSG-ineligible — revisit later)

### 3. Unique Value Proposition

> **"See your restaurant's new website tonight. Pay SGD 1,500 to ship it next week. No monthly fee unless you want one."**

The wedge: prospect gets a real, working preview of their actual restaurant before they're asked to commit. Compresses the entire "agency interview → mockup → quote → revisions" cycle into one tablet demo.

### 4. Solution (Concrete deliverables)
- AI-built 4-6 page mobile-first website (home, menu, reservations, contact, optional about/gallery)
- Google Business Profile linked / refreshed
- Mobile-first, <2-second load, structured data for SERP
- Embedded reservation link (Chope / Oddle / built-in form)
- Order-online link (FoodPanda / Grab / Oddle — no own checkout in v1)
- SG payment rail mentions where relevant (PayNow QR)
- Domain registration + 1-year hosting included
- 14-day post-launch support window
- Maintenance plan (SGD 79/mo) optional: unlimited menu updates, monthly review-response, GBP refresh

### 5. Channels (Ranked — see `01-discovery/raw/distribution.md`)
1. **Cold walk-ins with tablet demo** (months 1-3, founder-driven)
2. **Cold email to `info@`** (month 2-3 onward, after revenue funds compute)
3. **Instagram DM** (month 2 onward, complement to email)
4. **POS/reservation partnerships** (Oddle, Chope — month 2 onward, slow ramp)
5. **F&B industry events** (RAS, SBF — month 3 onward)
6. **PSG inbound** (post-approval, month 6+)

### 6. Revenue Streams
- **Primary:** SGD 1,000-1,500 one-time site fee (80%+ gross margin)
- **Secondary:** SGD 79/mo maintenance retainer (target 30%+ attach)
- **Tertiary:** Add-on services (photography pack SGD 300, English copywriting SGD 200, ordering checkout setup SGD 800)
- **Future:** PSG-driven inbound at SGD 3,000 list / SGD 900 net (post-approval)
- **Partner referral revenue:** 10-20% from Oddle/Chope reservations attached to delivered sites

### 7. Cost Structure
- **Variable:** AI tooling (Lovable/Cursor/v0): USD 50-100/site at scale ([Estimate]: founder needs to validate <SGD 2/site for cold-email economics to work)
- **Variable:** Hosting + domain per customer: ~SGD 30/year
- **Fixed:** Email tools (Instantly + Hunter): SGD 300/month total
- **Fixed:** Cloud + dev tools (Vercel/Supabase + AI subs): SGD 200/month
- **Fixed:** Tablet/transport for walk-ins: SGD 250/month
- **Fixed:** PSG application + accountant + insurance: SGD 500 one-time + SGD 100/month
- **Opportunity cost:** Founders' time at SGD 50/hr blended → ~10-15hr per closed customer

### 8. Key Metrics
- **Top-of-funnel:** spec sites generated/week, walk-ins/week, emails sent/week
- **Mid-funnel:** demo-to-pitch rate, reply/walk-look rate
- **Bottom-funnel:** **close rate per touch** (target: walk-in 10%, email 1%), **CAC** (target: ≤SGD 200 month 1, ≤SGD 100 month 6)
- **Revenue health:** **MRR from maintenance attach** (target: 30% attach by month 3), gross margin per site (target: 75%+)
- **Compute discipline:** **per-site generation cost** (target: ≤SGD 2 by month 2)

### 9. Unfair Advantage
- **None durable today.** This is the honest answer.
- **Temporary advantages (12-18 months):**
  1. **AI velocity** before incumbents (Verz, OOm) re-tool
  2. **Spec-site demo** before Wix Aria automates it
  3. **Local 2-person team** can do walk-ins SG agencies can't justify economically
- **Path to durable advantage:**
  - Become PSG-approved (12-18 months)
  - Build a verticalized SG restaurant-website playbook (menu OCR, PayNow integration, Oddle handshake) that horizontal AI tools won't replicate
  - First 50 named customers + case studies = local trust moat

## Strategic Choices Made

| Decision | Choice | Why |
|----------|--------|-----|
| Beachhead vertical | Restaurants (per founder) | Founder conviction + spec-site wedge most viscerally demonstrated for visual businesses |
| Beachhead geography | Singapore (per founder) | Founder location + sufficient market size |
| Pricing | SGD 1,000-1,500 one-time + SGD 79/mo optional | Founder's price thesis preserved + LTV ladder added |
| Channel priority | Walk-ins → email → partnerships | Walk-ins beat email at this ACV per research; partnerships are slow |
| AI tooling | Lovable / Cursor / v0 as primary stack | Best velocity for full-app delivery |
| PSG path | Apply in parallel, not delay launch | 6-12 month approval timeline; can't wait |
| Product depth | Full website + GBP, NOT ordering checkout v1 | Avoid scope creep; Oddle/FoodPanda already solve checkout |

---

## Flags

**Red Flags:**
- **Unfair advantage section is honest but weak.** Without PSG approval or proprietary tooling, every advantage erodes in 12-18 months. The play is to use the window to build local trust + named-customer moat before incumbents catch up.

**Yellow Flags:**
- **Maintenance attach rate (30%)** is an assumption with no SG-specific data. If only 5-10% attach, MRR ladder fails and the business stays one-time-revenue forever.
- **Per-site compute cost <SGD 2** is engineering-feasible but not proven for a 2-person team. Engineering spike required week 1.
