## Funding Needs

**No external funding required for Base scenario.** Founder's personal budget ($500-2K/mo × 6 months = $3K-12K) covers pre-launch one-time costs and early launch infra.

If the project tracks Optimistic and the founder wants to accelerate hiring:
- **Year 1.5 contractor budget:** $20-40K for part-time engineering / design — fundable from Y1 cash flow
- **Year 2 partner / co-founder dilution:** consider only if Y2 ARR exceeds $200K and growth bottlenecks are clearly skill-based

**Pre-seed / seed considerations:**
- The category (vertical-AI gaming companion) is fundable. Mobalytics raised $26M total before exit.
- But Pokémon-only TAM is small — investors will want to see a generalization plan (other competitive games?) before committing real capital.
- **Recommendation: bootstrap through Y2.** Revisit funding only if compounding metrics justify scale ambition.

## Runway Analysis

Founder's stated runway: 3-6 months "before reassessment."

**Reassessment milestones (sized to actual runway):**

| Runway used | Calendar (assuming May 2026 start) | Decision criteria |
|---|---|---|
| Month 3 (Aug 2026) | Mid-Worlds-prep | Closed beta tested, advisor secured, creator outreach in motion |
| Month 6 (Nov 2026) | Off-season Q2 | At least Base trajectory: 3,000+ Free signups, 50+ paid, MRR $400+ |
| Month 9 (Feb 2027) | Reg H ramp | Base or above: $2K+ MRR, 100+ paid, churn under 20% |
| Month 12 (May 2027) | Worlds 2027 ramp | Optimistic possible: $5K+ MRR clears Year 2 invest decision |

**Decision rule (kill criteria input for Phase 8):**
- If **Month 6** lands below Conservative (free signups <2,500 OR paid <30 OR MRR <$300), seriously consider pivoting or shutting down. The founder time is more valuable elsewhere.
- If **Month 6** is in Base territory, continue and focus on Q2 retention.
- If **Month 6** is Optimistic, double down — consider hiring contractor for v1.1 acceleration.

## Flags

**Red Flags:**
- None.

**Yellow Flags:**
- Conservative scenario is economically a loss. Founder must have a clear "kill at Month 6 if Conservative" mindset to avoid sunk-cost continuation.
- Off-season trough (Q2 Y1) is the highest-risk quarter. v1.1 ship discipline is critical.
- Year 2 and Year 3 projections assume retention holds and feature shipping cadence is maintained. Both are in the founder's hands.
- All scenarios assume Pivot 1 (tournament-prep persona) is correct. If real WTP comes from a different persona than assumed (unlikely but possible), all numbers must be re-derived.
